This project analyzes the historical crude oil price data based of a 12 month moving average. The research studies trends of oil prices based of the economic and politcal movements. This includes oil crises, financial recessions, and the COVID-19 pandemic. The results show that oil prices depend heavily on these events. Oil is a highly influenced market that can be changed on global supply and demands. By using this data we can visualize the data and we can see how economic factors play a role in oil prices.
