Oil is on of the most important resources in our economy. We constantly see the destruction and the desire for control over the oil markets. It’s price is based on supply, demand, and political events. Based on this idea we can identify key important events based of sudden increases and decreases in trends.

In this project, Historical data from when oil prices were first recorded to current time is analyzed using a 12 month moving average. The reason for this is to make the data easier to identify economic trends and shifts.

To understand the Oil market, I have compiled 7 different key economic and political moments that directly effect oil prices. This includes oil supply shocks, financial crises, and the COVID-19 pandemic. By analyzing these events we can see how the oil market is impacted
